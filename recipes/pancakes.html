Blueberry Flax Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes with ground flax seed and blueberries for a healthier, fiber filled pancake.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups dry pancake mix
  • 1/2 Cup flax seed meal
  • 1 Cup skim milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Set a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Stir together the pancake mix and flax seed meal.
  3. Whisk together the milk and eggs. Pour the liquid into the dry ingredients, and stir just until moistened.
  4. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the hot skillet. Sprinkle with as many blueberries as desired.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
